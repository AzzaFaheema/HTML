THRISSUR

Thrissur is a city in the South Indian state of Kerala. It's known for sacred sites and colourful festivals. In the center is Vadakumnathan Temple, dedicated to Lord Shiva and adorned with murals.

The top attraction to visit in Thrissur District are:

  • Athirappilly Waterfalls
  • Guruvayur Temple
  • Athirapilly Falls
  • Kauthuka Park
  • Snow Storm
